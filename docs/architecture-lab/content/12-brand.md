## Usage terms (summary)

### Do

- Use marks only to **accurately refer** to xAI or Grok services  
- Follow these guidelines and updates  
- Make changes if xAI requires after review  

### Don’t

- Imply endorsement, approval, or sponsorship  
- Use marks in app titles, domains, or product names **not** made by xAI  
- Alter logos or create a new mark by combining with other elements
