# Memory Glass · Product & ethos

The WebGrid/BCI one-liner was a **shipping wedge**, not the ceiling.  
This doc holds the **full aim** and still names what can ship without apology.

---

## Ethos (paradigm)

**The shift:** live data, live collaboration, and craft that is **shaped around a person** — from the IoT node on the planet, through the studio, the desk, the market, the fleet — not a one-size Chromium “new tab” or a Windows One skin.

Humanity’s next browser is not another page renderer.  
It is a **living instrument**: presence, prediction, performance, and production in one craft that can train robots, clone maker fleets, and stay personal.

---

## North-star sentence (breadth)

> **Memory Glass is a native, user-shaped living craft** — a collaborative browser-OS from edge device to business — where **live data, markets, music, training, multi-seat mesh, and a full language/jam keyboard plane** hop on in seconds, and **SLAM of person + craft** can train robots and spin **repeatable fleets of instances** for makers worldwide.

Not Electron. Not Chrome. Not “another start page.”  
Rust + WKWebView droplet · hot-pipe · mesh · markets · music · **keyboard/Braille/DDR/pattern-flow** · kbatch R4 · spatial.

### Keyboard / jam / patient plane (critical)

Grok is still **content** — you cannot yet *conduct* live music, conversation, and tracking as one flow.  
**qbpm** named the future jam studio. The MG keyboard + staff/notation + path geometry make the browser **live, breathe, and create**, with a path to **xAI voices**.

| Loop | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Play → BPS pill | WebGrid training floor |
| Type → path / ANALYZE | Pattern flow · kbatch shadows · 32-lang R4 |
| Braille / DDR | Access + BCI timing therapy |
| Jam → staff + qbpm-live | Live music iteration, not playlist chrome |
| Books / dojo | Living language plane |

**Neuralink patients + SpaceX/TerraFab chips:** same plane runs standalone or embedded — large targets, cross-language, exportable path/state — a **new product mark** off the living craft, not a Chromium fork.

See [`KEYBOARD-PLANE.md`](./KEYBOARD-PLANE.md).

### Grok calibration boot + flourish SHOW

Fastest path to **prove every touch surface**, then **show off** the craft:

| Phase | What | Entry |
|-------|------|--------|
| **CAL** | Open / act / close each menu surface (CTRL, search, keyboard+codec feeds, board, beats, field WG/Go/Chess, maze, GEO, bloch, raider, dragon) | `window.__mgCal.boot()` · `?mg_cal=1` · CTRL → **Calibrate** |
| **SHOW** | Only if CAL green — Play stack, codec live ASCII/HEX/BIN/GLYPH, Field modes, Beats+path, clear to lean | auto after CAL · or `?mg_cal=show` |
| **fast** | CAL only, ~50ms steps | `?mg_cal=fast` · `__mgCal.calibrate({fast:true})` |

Live health: `window.__mgMenus.probe()` · pill **MENUS n/n** · `~/.panda/mg-soak/watch/menu-health.jsonl` · `scripts/mg-menu-monitor.sh --status`.

## Pitch paragraph (30 seconds)

SpaceX went public. Markets flattened and democratized (Robinhood-scale AI flow, prediction markets, live games). Music wants terminal-grade live for DJs and training, not a playlist box. Collaboration wants Cursor/Grok-class **customize-to-you**, not enterprise defaults.  

Memory Glass aims to be the **craft that holds that stack**: a browser you **personalize to your market**, with **live SLAM** of you-and-tool, **shared mesh**, **trackable predictions** against streaming games and real markets, **ambient/live music instruments**, and a path from **one maker instance → a fleet of the same craft around the world**. kbatch R4-data is the language/school plane under it. WebGrid is the high-FPS training floor. Trading and prediction filmstrips are the money plane. Music staff + maze rain are the sonic plane. Collab mesh is the multi-seat plane.
